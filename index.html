Quick Summary

Last Updated: 2026-01-04

What We Do

The App helps you create AI-powered nail art designs, save them to your gallery, and share them in Discover if you choose.

Key Facts

  • Sign-in uses Apple or Google (Firebase Auth).
  • Designs are stored in Firebase Storage and Firestore.
  • AI generation runs through FAL.ai.
  • Subscriptions are managed via Apple/Google and RevenueCat.
  • Firebase Analytics is used for product insights.

Permissions

  • Photo Library: to save designs to your device.
  • No camera, microphone, location, or contacts access required.
Important: Designs shared to Discover and your display name become public. Consider this before sharing.
